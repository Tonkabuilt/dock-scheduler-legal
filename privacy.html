Dock Scheduler — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-25

Summary

Dock Scheduler, provided by Tonka Built, LLC (“we”, “us”), helps manage dock install scheduling and syncs with Jobber. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect from Jobber integrations, how we use it, and how you can contact us.

What we collect

  • Jobber account identifiers and metadata (accountId, account name).
  • Client and service property data (names, addresses, contact info).
  • Job data (job IDs, notes, scheduled times, statuses).
  • Scheduling data produced by the Service (assignments, travel calculations).
  • OAuth tokens necessary to access the Jobber API (stored securely).

How we use data

We use Jobber data strictly to provide scheduling, mapping, weather-aware adjustments, and synchronization features. We do not sell personal data to third parties. We may share data with third-party providers only to provide the service (for example, a weather API for forecasts).

Data security

We store tokens and user data securely. Access tokens and refresh tokens are kept encrypted and access to them is limited to systems that need them to operate the Service.

Data retention and deletion

On disconnection, we will mark the Jobber integration as disconnected. You may request deletion of stored data related to your Jobber account by contacting jake@tonkabuilt.com. We will delete or anonymize your data upon verified request within a reasonable timeframe.

User rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data that Dock Scheduler stores. Contact us at jake@tonkabuilt.com to exercise these rights.

Third-party services

We use third-party services (for example, weather APIs and geocoding services). These services have their own privacy policies. We only send them the minimum data necessary to perform their function (e.g., latitude/longitude for weather lookups).

Contact

Questions or requests about this Privacy Policy? Email jake@tonkabuilt.com.
